六朝金粉地，十里秦淮河

金陵，从时间深处走来，成了如今的南京。

Nanjing, as the “ancient capital of six dynasties and the capital of ten dynasties”

exudes a romantic and charming historical

and cultural atmosphere everywhere.

The old events of the Six Dynasties have gone with the flowing water,

but Nanjing’s mountains, rivers, bridges, alleys, and even an alley

that you pass through inadvertently still tell unusual legends.

Do you have a plot called: Want to go to Jinling to see?

01

Nanjing University

In the ancient capital of Jinling（Nanjing）

where it is beautiful and the tiger is on the dragon plate,

NTU（Nanjing University） is full of classical beauty

and the historical precipitation has given her a profound background.

Xianlin Campus has also moved Suzhou Gardens into the campus,

with the Suzhou Xiangxuehai as the prototype

and the theme of “Plum Blossom”

which is a beautiful scenery among the modern buildings on the campus of NTU.

This building, which is covered with ivy, is the landmark of NTU - the North Building.

It is the best witness of the years,

and countless people have left their green student memories here.

Whenever, wherever,

as long as you walk into NTU,

you will feel its beauty and variety.

02

Pioneer Bookstore (Wutaishan Branch)

As one of the top ten most beautiful in the world,

Pioneer Bookstore is located in the basement garage of Wutaishan Gymnasium,

with a white parking space line on the ground.

In this neglected and abandoned underground space,

Pioneer Bookstore has shaped a city’s cultural space

and has become one of Nanjing’s cultural cards.

Walking in the long aisle of the bookstore,

there is no partition in the traditional sense,

and there are books at your fingertips all around,

which invisibly creates a strong desire to read.

03

Confucius Temple Qinhuai River

When it comes to Nanjing, no one does not know Qinhuai River.

For thousands of years, the voices of people,

cruise ships, fishermen’s songs,

and endless stories of the ancient city have all flowed in the sound of paddles

and lights on the Qinhuai River.

Boating in Qinhuai, the ancient buildings with white walls and black tiles

on both sides of the river are constantly coming into view.

The lights on the buildings and the strings of red lanterns

under the eaves are reflected in the river water,

and you can feel the fireworks in the south of the old city.

Speciality cuisine

Food, it is not only the local people’s three meals a day,

but also presents a strong flavor of life in a bowl and a dish.

Mei Ling Porridge

A traditional dish in Nanjing. It is made of soy milk, glutinous rice, japonica rice, yam, lily, wolfberry, rock sugar and other ingredients.

It is rich in aroma, nourishing and appetizing, and rich in nutrition.

Drinking such a warm bowl of Meiling porridge on an autumn morning

will make the whole day full of energy!

Chicken Soup Bun

Nanjing’s soup dumplings neutralize the taste characteristics of the north and south.

It is sweet and salty, salty and fresh, and a little sweet,

but it is not as sweet as Wuxi and Suzhou, and it has Nanjing characteristics.

Red Bean Lantern

The red bean yuanxiao is thick and sweet, and it is a dessert with Nanjing characteristics.

Using red bean paste to cook small yuanxiao and lotus seeds,

and then thickening with lotus root starch,

it is really well-deserved to describe it as “soft, waxy and sweet”!

Nanjing Summer Mustard Garden Hotel

“One sentence is beautiful, and the city of Jinling is planted.”

There are so many places in Nanjing that I want to know and feel.

This time, let’s stay in Garden Hotel and open the city in a different way.

Mustard Seed Garden is the former “Jinling Famous Garden“,

which was the private residence of Li Yu, a famous writer in the late Ming and early Qing dynasties.

Today, it has been transformed into the “Nanjing Summer Mustard Garden Hotel”

on the basis of the old site.

It is located in Laomen East, hidden in the city,

quiet in the middle of the hustle and bustle.

As soon as you enter the door, the rockery, flowers and ponds are unique.

The pavilions and pavilions are scattered,

one step at a time, one scene, one feels like someone is in the middle of the painting.

The Summer Mustard Garden adopts the architectural style of the Qing Dynasty.

The antique buildings are equipped with intelligent facilities.

The three-row houses are planned together with the corridor design.

Some of the displays with a sense of age in the corners are more Added a touch of art.

The most surprising thing is the Kunqu Opera performance

in the Mustard Seed Garden, the waterfront stage,

the audience in the pavilion and the people in the play across the water.

A song A Dream in the Garden,

the people in the scene have a melodious Kunqun,

and the sleeves of the water are graceful,

and only a Kundi melodiously cooperates

and spreads across the water.

There is also a whisky bar “Zui Taiping” hidden in this Chinese garden hotel.

Here you can find both whisky from different countries and genuine cigars from places like Cuba.

You can watch the waterfall in the rain, watch the moon,

and listen to songbirds at night.

Walking through it, as if walking through the dust of history,

one brick, one tile, one flower and one tree, one drink, one meal, one bed and one seat,

watching mustard seeds and seeing Xumi,

enjoying traditional cultural classics and modern life The charm of artistic **.

From a whole new perspective

Discover the beauty of this world

This city has gone through too many ups and downs :

it has held up prosperity, witnessed changes, experienced pain…

Moldova - a unique European travel experience!

Posted on 2022-02-24 Edited on 2022-02-14

If you are tired of staying in the city all day,

looking up are all high-rise buildings;

I was repressed everywhere between life and work,

but I couldn’t shout loudly to vent the repression in my heart.

If you want to escape the hustle and bustle of the city,

find a quiet place where the restless heart can stay.

So, go to Moldova! ! !

As a traditional agricultural country,

Moldova attracts tourists with its natural and simple rural style.

Moreover, the local natural environment has never been polluted,

and one can feel the harmony and unity between man and nature.

Here you can escape from the hustle and bustle of the city

and let vacationers from afar enjoy Moldova to the fullest.

The ease of life in the countryside.

If you catch up with local festivals,

you can also sing, dance and have a good time with

the warm and friendly Moldovan people.

In addition to escaping the hustle and bustle of the city,

visit Moldova’s tourist attractions.

For example: you can go to the Orheiul Vechi cave monastery

overlooking the Raut river,

Or go to Curchi Monastery, an Orthodox church with religious symbols

and impressive paintings…

So, don’t hesitate to go to Moldova to experience it!

Wine tasting!

If you are a wine lover, then be sure to visit the local wine tasting

Moldova has a unique geographical environment

that can breed the best quality wines.

It has more than 5,000 years of winemaking history

and is one of the earliest countries in the world to make wine.

One of the most prestigious wineries, purcari,

has made the Queen of England choose wine here for five consecutive years.

The wine of this winery is the designated table wine

for some high-end people in Europe.

If you are in the local area, you can taste the unique taste of local wine

at a relatively favorable price!

If you want to feel the rich local wine culture,

it is best to visit the first weekend in October,

Come to Moldova for this wine festival!

The festival celebrates Moldova’s rich winemaking tradition.

During the festival, the winery will be very confident

and proud to show the carefully brewed wine to the guests who come to attend,

And provide tourists with the opportunity to taste for free.

In addition, several local restaurants and cafés serve traditional delicacies:

grilled meats, placinta (Moldovan cheese-filled pie),

other national delicacies, fruits and desserts.

If you want to experience a different Christmas, then go to Moldova!

Moldova celebrates Christmas from December 24th to 26th,

and preparations begin in November,

It included baking cakes and slaughtering pigs,

followed by three days of banquets and exchange of gifts interestingly.

Moldova celebrates Christmas twice a year:

Orthodox and Catholics follow different calendars,

The Catholic Church celebrates Christmas on December 25th, and the Orthodox Church celebrates Christmas on January 7th.

Most Moldovans believe in the Orthodox Church,

and Christmas on January 7 is relatively lively.

Christmas is the most romantic and charming time in Moldova.

Streets and shops are lit up and decorated,

thousands of festive lights come on one by one

The whole city is brightly lit, gleaming under the bright stars, creating a beautiful festive atmosphere.

The Christmas market dates back to the Middle Ages,

with hundreds of idyllic Christmas wooden houses

spread out along the sidewalks towards the square

Scattered throughout the city center,

a fairytale-like winter wonderland;

It attracts tourists who come and go and enjoy a variety of happiness

in the world of ice and snow.

At the Christmas market,

people can experience the oldest and purest traditions of Moldova,

In the hut, there are various Christmas-themed dolls,

china, garlands, handmade ornaments,

and Christmas delicacies such as gingerbread, sausages, and desserts.
