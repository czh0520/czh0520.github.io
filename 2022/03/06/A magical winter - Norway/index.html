A magical winter - Norway

Chen zhihong · 2022-3-6 · 次阅读

There's a kind of winter in this world that

we'd all like to call magic——the eternal night,

The gorgeous and mysterious aurora,

the mountains and forests are all covered in silver...

Norway has been immersed in such figurative magic

Speaking of Norway, we are always familiar and unfamiliar.

From the salmon in high-end grocery stores to the minimalist design inspiration of furniture

and daily necessities, many ingredients in life come from this country,

but how do these tiny bricks stack into the happiest habitat in the world,

The ideal country of mankind?

The Beatles, Haruki Murakami and Wu Bai

took turns to build our imagination of the “Norwegian Forest“,

but are these literary and artistic works the real Norway,

or is it just a beautiful misunderstanding?

We do often put Norway on our “must go” travel lists,

but we are often stuck with long journeys, high prices and viruses.

What does Norway consist of ? What is Norway?

King Harald V of Norway, gave the perfect answer:

Norway is full of lofty mountains and deep fjords.

As at the end of that speech, King Harald V also said:

But first of all, Norway is the people who live in this land.

Our beating heart is our home,

it is not determined by the borders of the country.

Norway is you, Norway is also us, this peace of mind is my hometown.

*It is a mountain plain and *

an offshore archipelago of countless islands and reefs.

It is lush fields and soft heath.

We found that for Norwegians,

there is nothing more beautiful than nature.

The most important thing in life is that

the balcony at home can see the fjord,

It is a wooden house where you can live in the wild on weekends,

ski in winter and go to sea in summer.

For tourists, the best way to experience the natural beauty of Norway is to take a boat,

wandering in the peaceful embrace of the fjord,

watching the green mountains reflect the water on both sides,

glacier waterfalls flying down from time to time,

scattered on the shore of the wooden houses and The village,

like a house in a fairy tale world, is so beautiful that it is not like the human world.

The word “Norway” has the meaning of “the road to the north”.

The ingenious work of ancient glaciers has fragmented the coastline here;

Warm ocean currents poured into the land,

bringing thousands of islands to Norway,

Steep and winding fjords are also left behind.

It injects a bit of adventure into the Norwegian character ——the 8th century AD,

the Viking adventures set out from the bay here.

At the end of the 19th century,

the explorer Friedrich Joff Nansen crossed the Arctic Ocean aboard the “Frame”,

and first confirmed that the North Pole is a sea.

Eighteen years later, Roald Amundsen came to the other side of the world

and became the first conqueror of the South Pole.

Ibsen also wrote in a letter to a friend:

“Only when you find your way to the mountains and

fjords will you appreciate the beauty of this country.

Growing up in a country like Norway,

the Northern Lights danced across blue and black skies in winter,

the whole sky seemed to be on fire,

illuminating snow-covered mountaintops and

the Milky Way gleaming with stars.

I was sitting on a quiet and lonely snowy mountain, and that was

when I got close to nature and became interested in it,

and I began to ponder the wonders of the universe,

the stars, nature and human beings.

Here, those who wear old windbreakers and wooden skis may be worth hundreds of millions.

Those who are busy doing farm work,

painting walls and moving bricks on weekends

are most likely to be senior white-collar workers during the week.

When the French are dying for a touch of caviar,

the Norwegians may be fishing;

when the British try to set standards for tea drinking,

the Norwegians may be fishing;

when the Italians are dancing and chatting,

the Norwegians may be fishing alone .

Abandon vanity and exaggeration, return to practicality and simplicity,

and not be burdened by life, in order to enjoy the beauty of life.

Luxury may not really improve happiness.

When material desires are reduced,

there will naturally be more space for the mind,

and you will not be willing to spend some money,

so you can spend money on travel to treat yourself.

Excessive enthusiasm, asking for help, maybe not really friendly.

Maintain courteous social distancing, but be sincere,

and you will be more aware of being alone with yourself.

Although Norway is extremely beautiful, it is also extremely cold,

and there are extreme days and nights, and

the Norwegians’ philosophy of survival is that

the more they are in such an environment,

the more they must maintain inner calm and peace.
