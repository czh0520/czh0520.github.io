Loneliness is a person's carnival

Chen zhihong · 2022-3-22 · 次阅读

In this impetuous and prosperous world
more and more like being alone，more and more silent
The first half of my life has gone through the vicissitudes of life

After seeing the human beings and emotions in the world

I love being alone

Being alone is the best way to live

Being alone is also a kind of enjoyment,

a kind of freedom and a kind of peace and tranquility

Being alone is not an escape, not cowardice, but a readjusted lifestyle

must love yourself

Learn to be alone and watch the flowers bloom

laugh at the situation

For the rest of my life, use the most beautiful mentality,

the most elegant temperament to understate the solitude…

When we grow up

there are some things that

we would rather write in our own memos than tell others.

Once you have your own independent space,

your body and mind are reluctant to waste a minute and a second outside,

and looking out the window alone feels the most enjoyable.

Going to the library alone, in a daze, shopping alone, and not looking for friends,

even if you are holding a mobile phone,

you will be swiping TikTok and not chatting about news.

Every step of the transformation is expressing the natural and true life of life.

Perhaps, only when people are alone can they clear the fog,

wander the mind beyond the material,

communicate with the spirit of heaven and earth, and see the truth of life.

Because being alone can relieve a person’s spiritual burden, restore the soul and self, and sublimate the soul.

One：Being alone is a relief for the mind

People spend a lot of time in useless socializing,

or have busy schedules for themselves.

In fact, most of the time,

I am afraid that if I don’t do it, I may lose something.

Under a high level of mental stress,

it is easy for us to live more and more confused.

Thoreau once said: The more things a man can put aside, the richer he is.

A person who can let go of all the hustle and bustle in his alone time,

at least in spirit, must be truly rich.

Free up your heart and time and enjoy your time alone.

When you are alone, you are close to yourself

and it is easier to listen to your inner voice.

The more people who don’t care about gains and losses,

the higher the level, they are good at being alone,

and when they are away from crowds,

they also look like they are beyond the reach of others.

It is said that if gains and losses are entangled in the brain

and weighed down on the heart, life cannot be peaceful.

In fact, whether you gain or lose,

they will not change because you think too much.

Turn off your computer,

throw your phone away,

and try a long, sedentary life.

Only by looking at your life from the perspective of a third party

can you **understand yourself better, understand the meaning of life’s gains **

and losses, and see life more clearly.

Two：Solitude is the return of the soul

It seems that each of us will have such a stage,

thinking that it is our own inner instinct to integrate into the world of others.

But after the baptism of time,

I found that being alone is the desire in my heart.

I saw a passage:

You stand in the hustle and bustle of the crowd

and look up at the gorgeous fireworks,

but I see the loneliness shining in your eyes.

The excitement belongs to others, and your loneliness is so dazzling.

I’ve had this feeling before, and I’m sure you have too.

People always prefer to participate in a lot of activities

that can’t explain the meaning, but do not want to be alone.

When the carnival crowd dispersed,

what was left in my heart was endless loneliness and desertion.

Philosopher Pascal said:

Almost all our suffering comes from our inability to be alone in a room.

Being alone turns out to be an ability.

You are always different in the eyes of outsiders

and who you are when you are alone.

One pretended to be indifferent, the other was at a loss.

How to make the self when alone, really calm and stable, this is a practice that

requires effort.

When a person reaches a certain stage,

he will always know his likes and dislikes.

If he likes it, he will enjoy it to the fullest,

and if he hates it, he will not do it at all.

Don’t care about the strange looks from others

when you are eating alone or walking alone.

In fact, the world is our own and has nothing to do with others,

and being alone is the most enjoyable pleasure that each of us should enjoy.

Even if it’s boring, take it on your own,

don’t involve others, and don’t feel uneasy.

When we settle ourselves well, we can be quiet and light,

allowing us to find our inner strength,

return to ourselves, and return to the most authentic life.

Three：Solitude is the sublimation of the soul

In this life, people are nothing more than knowing themselves,

refining themselves, and voluntarily transforming themselves.

Being alone is not loneliness, it is a choice.

Although it is not lively,

it is full of wisdom and is the best way for

everyone to know themselves spontaneously.

In the time of solitude, do the present things earnestly and down-to-earth,

without getting lost in the past or fearing the future.

Don’t be in a hurry to let life give you all the answers,

just have a little patience, those good things will always come

when you don’t expect them.

A person’s life can be dull and stagnant,

or it can be a full and wonderful adventure.

It’s only when you’re alone that you become more sure of what you really want.

Only when people learn to listen to their inner voice

can they discover a better self and live more deeply.

The time that can really add value and sublimation to oneself

comes from the time alone.

Only when we are alone can we fully please ourselves,

enjoy our time, and find meaning in our lives.

People who know how to enjoy being alone can live a boring life to the fullest,

live out life as it really is, and meet a better self.

Don’t be afraid to be alone, don’t refuse to be alone.

Because that time when you can blend in with prosperity

and enjoy being alone is actually the best time in your life.
