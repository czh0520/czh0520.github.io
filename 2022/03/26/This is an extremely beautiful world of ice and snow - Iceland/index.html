This is an extremely beautiful world of ice and snow - Iceland

Chen zhihong · 2022-3-26 · 次阅读

A country of breathtaking beauty

one of the most magical places on earth

On this side of the galaxy!

However, the beautiful bays of this country,

Sleeping volcanoes and tourists

on the cover of brochures blown by the sea breeze are more beautiful

The natural scenery there is truly breathtaking and awe-inspiring.

Iceland is also a high-welfare developed country.

In Iceland, nationals have benefits

such as free education and free medical care provided by the state,

Housing is also free, and most workers receive higher wages.

However, for those who love to travel,

Iceland must be an incredible country!

It has the uncanny workmanship of nature,

It can even take you to witness the beauty of the aurora dancing in the night sky.

Iceland’s natural beauty and culture can be said to be unique in the world.

Many people list Iceland as a “must visit in this lifetime!”

One： Exploring the Blue Ice Cave

Fantastic pure natural glacier crystal palace

Witness the magic of nature and the most crystal blue

on earth in your lifetime!

There are many natural wonders in Iceland,

the first and most irreplaceable in the world is the Blue Ice Cave.

They are naturally formed, and in summer,

glacier meltwater flows through the bottom of the glacier,

and over time, it carves ice caves of varying shapes.

Glaciers are natural structures with a history of thousands of years,

and the air and impurities in the ice are all pressed out of the ice.

When there are no impurities in the ice body,

the light will not be refracted arbitrarily when passing through,

and the ice body will be more transparent.

A dreamy blue! !

After the discovery of the “natural beauty without makeup”,

Lan Bingdong quickly became the new favorite of the major travel media around the world.

Let landscape photographers and travel enthusiasts all over the world flock to it,

It has become an alternative “ice-cold” proposal destination

for couples to pursue romance.

Leave the most precious memory in your life!

Iceland’s blue ice caves ebb and flow with the seasons and temperatures.

As a pure natural glacier product,

its existence completely depends on the existence of glacier.

Global warming means the retreat of glaciers

and the demise of ice caves are inevitable

In a few decades, the most beautiful ice blue on earth may be extinct.

Therefore, when you travel to Iceland,

you should not miss the fading beauty of nature

and catch the last train of the glacier.

It is hoped that such a scene will serve as a warning and hope to everyone,

who will contribute to protecting the earth’s environment and saving glaciers.

Two： Hunt for the Northern Lights

The magical aurora that the solar wind “blows” to the earth,

feel the wonders of the universe beyond the earth.

Watching the beauty of the Northern Lights dancing in the night sky

is the lifelong dream of many people.

Many people come to Northern Europe in winter just to get

a glimpse of the Northern Lights.

The whole island of Iceland is in the aurora observation belt,

which can be described as the best place to observe the aurora in the northern hemisphere.

And there are so many features in winter in Iceland,

hunting aurora can be combined with other projects,

The itinerary will be richer and more fun.

Hot springs

Soak in hot springs in freezing weather

experience a different kind of wonderland

and beautify your skin!

Iceland is one of the countries with the most natural hot springs in the world,

and Icelanders have made hot springs a national leisure.

Thermal water is warm and comfortable and has many benefits for the skin.

In addition to natural hot springs,

Iceland also has hot spring pools all over the country.

Icelanders in every family go to the pool at least once a week,

so the pool is also an important place to socialize.

The two most famous hot springs in Iceland are one in the south and the other in the north

with the Blue Lake in the south and Lake Myvatn in the north.

The fairy spirit of Blue Lake makes people fascinated

The Mihu Hot Spring is also blue,

which is not inferior to the more famous Blue Lake.

In short, when you come to Iceland

you must experience hot springs,

which is the most Icelandic way of leisure.

Become a photographer and come to Iceland to take pictures of wonders

The most beautiful scenery around the corner

——Iceland is full of scenery, the nature is wild enough,

the house is cute enough, come to Iceland to become a photographer in seconds.

Iceland is famous for its natural scenery.

When you travel to Iceland, you must take home some decent scenery.

Iceland can be said to be full of scenery.

Traveling in Iceland, everyone can become a photographer

and shoot a blockbuster.

And Iceland’s popular and niche attractions are very photogenic,

There are countless extreme scenic spots along the No. 1 Ring Road.

Waterfalls, beaches, small towns, and strange rocks are never monotonous.

When it is sunny,

it is a crystal blue, and when it is cloudy,

it is a lake of cold silver and gray.

into the movie scene

The new favorite of Hollywood American dramas,

the scene of the alien scenery and the end of the world.

Coming to Iceland is like going to outer space.

Three： Experience Icelandic Culture in Reykjavik

The northernmost capital in the world,

the Nordic avant-garde minority cultural center

Iceland’s natural beauty is unparalleled,

and Iceland’s culture is also worth pondering.

Reykjavik is the northernmost capital in the world,

and the “most” of Reykjavik is by no means limited to geographical coordinates.

It is the undisputed center of Icelandic food, art, history and more.

Reykjavik best represents Iceland’s edgy and unique culture.

Here you can find authentic Scandinavian cuisine,

live music performances year-round, art galleries and museums,

and a vibrant nightlife.

Various festivals, big and small, further demonstrate

the city’s strong vitality and diverse values.

In Reykjavik, you will experience

the most peaceful city life in the world,

the most equal gender relations,

and the most open attitude towards sexuality.

In addition to the experience, I believe that the urban atmosphere

here can also benefit you a lot and become a better version of yourself！！！
