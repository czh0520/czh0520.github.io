The more free and easy you want to live, the more you have to learn to love yourself

Chen zhihong · 2022-3-1 · 次阅读

The older you are, the more you realize.

In this life, mentality is really important.

If you don’t owe others, you will not be coerced by external things;

if you don’t wrong yourself, you can protect yourself for the rest of your life;

also have the sobriety not to feel wronged, this kind of life is just right

“One of my big criteria for happiness is

how often I can say ‘no’ to a lot of things I don’t like.

Saying ‘no’ is a very happy thing,

because you don’t have to force yourself into a quagmire that you can’t get out of.

We take it for granted, and in our lives,

we always want to be recognized by others.

So unconsciously, I will try to please others,

do some things I don’t like, and even try to be perfect at the expense of grievances.

Life is too tense and tiring.

As I grow older, I slowly realize that

the most important thing is never others, but myself.

**Instead of living in the eyes and evaluations of others, **

it is better to live your own life well.

Don’t flatter others, don’t wrong yourself, don’t live up to the rest of your life,

this is the most comfortable way of living, the most comfortable way of life!

In the novel “Brave New World”, there is a very poignant sentence:

What people suffer is not that they replace thinking with laughter,

but that they don’t know why

they are laughing and why they are not thinking anymore.”

Sometimes it’s not the day-to-day trivialities that

make you feel tired, numb, and bored.

Instead, in pleasing others time and time again, he quietly lost himself.

In the book "Silent Confession",

the 16-year-old girl Lydia's appearance to please her parents makes people feel really distressed. Lydia's mother's biggest regret in her life was that she could not become a female doctor because of marriage and childbirth.

Her father felt pity because his skin color was out of tune with everything around him and couldn't fit into the group. In order to become her mother's ideal female doctor, Lydia forced herself to study subjects she didn't like, and did physics problems that she was not good at.

At the same time, in order to please her father, she will create the illusion that she has many friends and gets along well with them.

In fact, she had no friends at school. Under her favor, her mother mistakenly thought that she wanted to be a female doctor too, so she filled all her time and forbid her to do anything unrelated to her studies.

And her father also thought that she wanted to be a gregarious person, and he would tirelessly ask her and her friends about her situation every day, and used his method to help Lydia become a popular person.

The life in front of her made Lydia breathless. She didn't want to please anyone anymore, she just wanted to live for herself. But she had already adapted to the life of pleasing her parents, but she didn't know how to make herself happy.

Unable to bear all of this, she chose to commit suicide by throwing herself into the lake in the dead of night.

Like family, so is love and friendship.

There is no need to please those who have you in their hearts,

and it is useless to please those who do not have you in their hearts.

A lot of times, you try your best to please, but it may not be liked by others.

Still in the flattery again and again, I lose myself,

make myself scarred, and make sacrifices cheap.

Any relationship is never something you can have by being submissive and flattering.

As the saying goes:

*”People who can really appreciate you will always appreciate your proud appearance, *

not your pretended humble and lovable appearance.”

Instead of spending a lot of time and energy trying to please others,

it is better to spend a lot of time trying to please yourself and meet a better self.

In life, we have more or less put the emotions and thoughts of others

on top of others, while ignoring our own feelings.

Afraid of a dispute,

I learned to compromise, and I didn’t want to hurt the friendship,

so I chose to make it happen.

I would rather swallow my pain,

hide my grievances, and make wedding clothes for others,

and I don’t dare to say “no” boldly.

In the compromises again and again,

I gave others the opportunity to “bully” you at any time,

making myself more and more aggrieved.

In fact, interacting with people is like putting on shoes.

You can’t hurt your feet for fear of losing a pair of shoes that don’t fit.

A good relationship will never make you deeply wronged,

and the feelings that make you feel wronged will not last long.

Life is too short. When you encounter something you like, accept it calmly.

When you encounter something you don’t like,

you reject it decisively. Don’t feel wronged.

But in life, not everyone can be open-minded,

see clearly, be open-minded, and live out themselves bravely.

Many people, because of the trials and tribulations in the first half of their lives,

have endured all the hardships, suffered all the injuries, and shed tears.

In order not to repeat the same mistakes,

the second half of my life lived carefully, walking on thin ice.

As a result, let yourself live in the midst of troubles and losses,

and pass by all the good things in life.

As the saying goes, everything in the past is a prologue.

The love and hate, bitterness and tiredness of the first half of your life,

let it turn into a wisp of blue smoke drifting away in the wind,

don’t let it become a stumbling block to a better life in the rest of your life.

As the saying goes:

“When you are in love, you live up to people,

when you play, you live up to the scenery,

when you sleep, you live up to the bed,

and when you are alone, you live up to yourself.”

In a moment of floating life, a journey of landscapes and rivers for a year,

we finally came to the world, how can we easily fail the rest of our lives.

For the rest of your life, please pack up your mood,

live a good life, be kind to yourself,

and don’t let down the self who has gone through half a lifetime of hard work.

In the vast sea of people and people coming and going,

we have all deliberately catered to others and wronged ourselves to do all kinds of unwilling things.

Under the hardships of the years,

he hid the life he longed for in the depths of his heart,

and repeated every day according to the rules,

thus living up to the rest of his life.

But no matter what,

**you have to work hard to say goodbye to the lifestyle you don’t like, **

and go to the distance you yearn for.

Walking and walking, I gradually understood that

the most comfortable way of life in the second

half of life is not only the family and the lights;

it is three meals for two people, four seasons, flowers before the moon,

watching the flowers and watching the moon;

Just sit and enjoy the wine;

or, in every ordinary day, don't flatter others,

don't wrong yourself, live up to the rest of your life, and live a hot life.
