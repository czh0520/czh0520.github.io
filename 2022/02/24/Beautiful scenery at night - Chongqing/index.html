Beautiful scenery at night —— Chongqing

 · 2022-2-24 · 次阅读

No night view

don’t arrive in Chongqing

The night view of Chongqing is known

as the “Million Dollar Night View“.

Chongqing, the city of Internet celebrities? Magic City?

maybe

This mysterious mountain city with beautiful landscapes

and a long history has more of a fiery smell of fireworks.

And Chongqing under the night is the most lively

down-to-earth and most flesh-and-blood place

Here is the hottest hot pot,

The most beautiful night view, the most stunning beauty,

The most dazzling traffic and

The best life...

This is a city that many people must visit in their lifetime

It is also a beautiful night city that makes people "look for thousands of years"

Chongqing night, cool enough!

The night returner and the disco youth pass by by mistake

The elderly walking in the courtyard and the passing traffic

Roadside stalls collide with the hustle and bustle of bars…

Whenever night comes

the dormant city of Chongqing begins to be “restless”,

and even the passing car lights seem to have a sense of wanton pleasure.

The night in Chongqing has a unique and charming temperament.

Once the sky darkens,

the neon spreads all the way from the riverside to the city center,

from the lights of Wanjia all the way to the streets and alleys,

the whole city of Chongqing becomes lively and warm.

At night, the river bank is colorful and the lights are dazzling.

Two River Cruises circumnavigate the Yangtze River and Jialing River,

and start a 360° business trip.

The lights of Hongya Cave are as bright as stars,

overlapping and overlapping,

as if the movie has moved into reality.

The major bridges are also full of neon, printed with sparkling water,

which is very charming.

Even the warm yellow lights on the overpass

and the lights of passing vehicles

make the night in Chongqing particularly beautiful.

There is no night view without Chongqing!

In addition to the night scene in Chongqing that looks like a painting screen,

the fiery and lively fireworks of the world

are the aesthetics of this magical city.

In Chongqing at night, it will never be quiet.

It is the real Chongqing that is lively and noisy.

*There are people who are scurrying around in the night market to find delicious food, *

*some people drink tea and watch a play in the night teahouse, *

some people play in the river and play cards, and there are people who dance in the bar to decompress and hilarious…

All in all, Chongqing at night is the most lively and fragrant.

Chengnan Night Market Street

This night market in the south of the city,

before it got dark, it started to get lively.

Snacks, fruits, clothing, barbecue, night beer, vegetable stalls…

At a glance, there are dazzling, eat, drink and play everything.

The later it gets, the more lively the night market gets.

At night, the charcoal fire on the stove wafted up the smoke,

and people who had been busy all day filed into the venue.

The cries of the street vendors, the sizzling sound of deep-fried frying,

and the hilarious sound of conversations with people… are staged at the same time,

and all the fatigue gradually dissipated in the laughter.

There are also Danzishi Night Market, Jiaochangkou Night Market, Guanyinqiao Night Market,

Lianglukou Night Market…

These places marked “Night Market” are real night paradises.

As a gourmet food.

Chongqing people’s sense of ritual to start nightlife always starts with food.

Whether it’s a business district, a night market, a bar street, or an online celebrity check-in place…

The “ghost food” in Chongqing at night is very popular.

Chongqing’s nighttime food card is always the Jiugongge Hotpot.

At the bottom of the pot with red oil and various peppers,

the hot steam rolls roll with a rich spicy fragrance.

There are thousands of ingredients to choose from, such as hairy belly, fat intestines, seafood, vegetables… t

he more you eat, the more hearty you will be!

Chongqing Noodles

Take a sip of the fragrant, spicy, numb,

and refreshing Chongqing noodles,

and wait for the taste to slowly burst on the tip of the tongue, which is really refreshing。

Grilled fish

Spicy and fragrant, the grilled fish with crispy skin and tender meat is also addicting.

In one bite, the smooth and tender fish is wrapped in spicy soup to stimulate the taste buds,

and it is hard to stop eating it!

Chuanchuanxiang

Those chuanchuan hidden in the streets and alleys of Chongqing,

the incense is even more difficult to walk.

Put a large number of delicious and tender skewers into a red-hot pot and cook,

as the heat rises, the atmosphere of the night gradually warms up.

fireworks in the night
