Travel - Pursue the unknown of life

Posted on 2022-02-24 Edited on 2022-02-19 Views:

We need to travel. If we don’t touch the unknown,

our senses will become dull, our world will be so small,

and even curiosity will disappear.

Our eyes will no longer be on the distant horizon,

and our ears will not hear those familiar sounds.

Find the meaning of life

It also limits our experience of life

when we are satisfied with the life in front of us day after day.

When we finally understand one day,

we will find that the life we live together is at the cost of the dreams we once had.

Don’t allow yourself to be such a person. Fear of the unknown,

nostalgia for comfort will prevent us from being a traveler on an adventurous journey.

However, when you make such a choice, you will never regret it.

Of course, when you’re alone, on the open road, in the freezing rain,

or sick in an unfamiliar bed, you may be a little skeptical.

But in the end, you’ll be happier.

What is all the hardship and hardship compared to the knowledge you have gained

and the life you have lived through.

I like a life full of uncertainty, and I like my dynamic self.

Life is so short, even if it only blooms for an instant,

it is better than the monotonous withering day after day.

Most of us are busy at two o’clock and one line, small as dust, humble as dirt.

But because of this, everyone who lives seriously deserves a better version of themselves.

Every journey, every story, every person is waiting to make life so extraordinary.

We are all still young, we have many dreams and many frustrations.

Try to be better

I know that I have a lot of imperfections.

Afraid of the dark, afraid of insects, afraid of being hurt.

I am afraid that in the future, I will be alone.

But dear self, you have to learn to let go of the timid and inferior you,

look at the scenery outside, meet different people,

and make diamonds in the glass heart, so beautiful and strong.

Traveling, in my narrow view, is probably about leaving a familiar place

and experiencing the cultural environment of another place.

Its main purpose is to relax and learn some new things in unfamiliar places.

Because everyone relaxes in a different way,

the meaning of travel is different for everyone.

For example, for photography enthusiasts, the meaning of travel may be to capture pleasing scenery;
for food lovers, the meaning of travel may be to find unique snacks;
for history lovers, the meaning of travel may be to experience the culture left in the city;
friends circle hobbies For those, the meaning of travel may be to take pictures of pleasing scenery, find unique snacks, and feel the culture left in the city.

Travel - freedom like the wind, to see different beauty

After all, the meaning of travel is not to regret, but to enjoy

Yes, you can see a lot of things you have never seen before while traveling

Animals that were once only on TV,

the graceful giraffes on the African savannah, the bear-like lion cubs

They’re beautiful, they’re more dynamic than you can imagine, and they’re mind blowing

You can also see the dramatic sunrise at Angkor Wat

You can also feel the calm and warm evening under the snow-capped mountains in Mexico

You can see devout believers in front of the Wailing Wall in Jerusalem

You can do a lot of things that other people want or can’t think of,

you can do a lot of things that other people don’t like but you love,

and you can do things you’ve never done, things you can’t even think about,

I can’t tell you what travel means to us,

in the desolate and remote spiral arms of the Milky Way, in a tiny solar galaxy,

on a tiny blue planet, as insignificant as dust,

what can we do to travel on this tiny world significance?

In the long river of time in the universe,

what is the meaning of our life of only a hundred years?

We are too small, not only travel is meaningless, but all our actions,

our entire human actions, may be meaningless.

But in this desolate and remote galaxy, in our short life, in this only

Do what you want to do once in your life and be who you want to be that’s the biggest meaning

Travel is a mental adjustment that allows you to see a better world.

This sentence is correct, because when we are very impetuous or have a lot of troubles,

travel becomes one of the ways to solve troubles.

We can look at this beautiful world and change to a different one.

environment, to think about problems, and to live in a different environment,

we may calm down and think about more things,

so that we can really solve a lot of worries after we come back.

Embrace the world and come to terms with yourself

I have asked myself countless times, why do I want to travel, and I am not good if I don’t travel.

Slowly, I always feel that life has no passion,

and I don’t know the meaning of life.

I always fall into self-doubt, doubt the world, doubt the plight of life.

When I step out and let myself go out and embrace the world every week,

or a corner of the city, or a small town around the city,

or out of the province, but I still haven’t found the meaning of travel in

Where is the meaning of life, but I have no previous doubts,

I began to fall in love with this world, full of interest in all the unknowns of this world,

and want to explore the wonderful things of this world.

If you ask me what the meaning of travel is,

I still don’t understand it, but it seems that

I suddenly know a better self and walk towards a “road of no return”.
